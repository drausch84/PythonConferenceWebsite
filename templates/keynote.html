Denver Writer's Conference Keynote Speakers

Nathan Richardson

A well known author who specializes in medical thrillers and mystery novels, Nathan Richardson has earned his fair share of critical acclaim. Many critics have praised Richardson's descriptive characters and well-paced plots of his novels which has earned him two Edgar Awards. His catalogue includes the Beneath the Red Gown medical thriller series, What the Crows Saw, Veil of the Maidens, and The Deepest Grave.

Shayna Williams

Well-known for her satirical take on modern America, Shayna Williams has been described by the NY Times as the next Kurt Vonnegut. Her recent novel, A Rose by Any Other Name is a Weed, has earned her acclaim from numerous outlets, including the Chicago Tribune which praised the novel as "achingly truthful while maintaining a light-hearted edge".
