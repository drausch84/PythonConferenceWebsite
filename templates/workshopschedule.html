Denver Writer's Conference Workshop Schedule

Hone Your Skills in Our Workshops

Our workshops are where the magic happens! Take advantage of the opportunity to receive advice and critiques from our many participating experts. Have you felt that your story's characters needed to be fleshed out further? We have a workshop for that! Have you felt that your mystery novel lacks tension? We have a workshop for that! Do you have a world in your mind that you want to share with the rest of the world but are unable to find the words to give it detail? We have a workshop for that! We offer three different workshops in each session of the day for the Denver Writer's Conference, so be sure to take a look at the workshop schedule to decide which workshop works best for you.
